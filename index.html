Independent Software Studio

We build software that gets out of your way.

Small, focused tools for real everyday problems. No bloat, no subscriptions you forgot about, no accounts you don't need. Just software that works, quietly, in the background of your day.

What we believe

01

One job, done well

We don't build do-everything suites. Every tool we ship solves one specific problem, completely, and then gets out of the way.

02

Instant, not eventual

If a tool takes longer to use than to just do the task by hand, we didn't build it right. Speed is a feature, not an afterthought.

03

Privacy by default

Local-first and offline-first wherever we possibly can. Your data is yours, and we're not in the business of collecting it.

04

Own it, don't rent it

We'd rather sell you something once than bill you forever for it. Software should respect your wallet the same way it respects your time.

What we're building

We're a small, independent studio currently developing a lineup of lightweight Windows utilities, each one aimed at a single everyday annoyance, built to install in seconds and then be forgotten about, because it just works. More on the way soon.
